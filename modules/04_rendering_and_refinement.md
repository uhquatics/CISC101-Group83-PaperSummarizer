# Module 4: Rendering & Refinement

## Purpose
Produce final structured output with consistent formatting.

## Workflow
1. Compile paper summary.
2. Compile section-by-section summaries.
3. Generate expert vs lay summary table.
4. Create mini-glossary.
5. Insert checks & warnings.

## Formatting Rules
- Use GitHub-flavored Markdown.
- Ensure consistent headings and bullet points.
- Expert summaries: technical, precise.
- Lay summaries: simplified, accessible.

## Constraints
- No duplication of content.
- Maintain clarity across all sections.
